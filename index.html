Conditions We Treat

Bringing clarity to vision disorders with innovative, non-invasive therapy.

Amblyopia (Lazy Eye)

A condition where one eye is weaker than the other, often due to abnormal visual development in early childhood.

Bynocs Therapy: Digital therapy programs that stimulate the weaker eye and promote binocular vision recovery, suitable even for adults.

Strabismus (Squint or Crossed Eyes)

A misalignment of the eyes where they point in different directions. This can affect depth perception and lead to amblyopia.

Bynocs Therapy: Encourages alignment through binocular training exercises, improving coordination and visual comfort.

Digital Eye Strain (DeStrain)

Caused by prolonged screen use, leading to symptoms like dry eyes, headaches, blurred vision, and fatigue.

Bynocs Therapy: Personalized digital solutions that reduce visual stress and support healthy screen usage habits.

Neuro-Ophthalmology

Treating complex conditions involving both the eyes and the brain. Bynocs collaborates with specialists to support conditions like optic neuropathy, double vision, and more.
